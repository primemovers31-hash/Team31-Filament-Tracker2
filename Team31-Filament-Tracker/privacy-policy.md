# Privacy Policy - Team31 Filament Tracker

Team31 Filament Tracker is an open-source app for managing filament spool inventory.

## Data

- The app may store spool inventory data in Firebase when cloud sync is enabled.
- Google sign-in may be used for authentication.
- Local data may also be stored on-device for offline use.

## Account Deletion

To request account deletion, open an issue at:

https://github.com/primemovers31/Team31-Filament-Tracker/issues
